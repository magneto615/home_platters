About the Art

Each Forever Home Portrait is lovingly hand-painted on premium ceramic platters. Using fine-tipped brushes and permanent ink, I carefully sketch and paint every architectural detail of your home, from the windows and doors to the landscaping and unique features that make it yours.

These custom portraits make perfect gifts for housewarmings, anniversaries, weddings, or any milestone moment. They're not just decorative pieces—they're heirlooms that capture the emotion and memory of a special place.

Every platter is signed, dated, and includes the family name, making it a truly personalized work of art.
