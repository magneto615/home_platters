About the Art

Each Home Painted Platter is lovingly hand-painted on premium ceramic platters. Using fine-tipped brushes and permanent ink, I carefully sketch and paint every architectural detail of your home, from the windows and doors to the landscaping and unique features that make it yours.

These custom home portraits make perfect gifts for housewarmings, anniversaries, weddings, or any milestone moment. They're not just decorative pieces—they're heirlooms that capture the emotion and memory of a special place.

Every platter is signed, dated, and includes the family name, making it a truly personalized work of art.

How to Order

01

Submit Your Photo

Send a clear photo of the home you'd like painted. Front views work best, but any angle can work.

02

Choose Your Platter

Select your preferred platter shape and size. Share any special details you'd like included in the portrait.

03

Receive Your Art

I'll hand-paint your custom portrait and carefully package it for delivery. Each piece takes 3-4 weeks to complete.

Care Instructions

Hand Wash Only: Clean gently with mild soap and warm water. Pat dry with a soft cloth.

No Dishwasher: Do not place in the dishwasher as high heat may damage the artwork.

Not Food-Safe: These pieces are not food-safe. Designed to be decorative only. Not microwave-safe.
